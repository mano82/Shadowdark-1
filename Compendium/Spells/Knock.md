# [[Knock|Knock]]

>[!summary]
> *Tier* 2
> Wizard
> *Duration*: Instant
> *Range*: Near
> 
> *Source:* Core

A door, window, gate, chest, or portal you can see within range  instantly opens, defeating all  mundane locks and barriers.  

This spell creates a loud knock  audible to all within earshot.
