# [[Fixed-Object|Fixed Object]]

>[!summary]
> *Tier* 2
> Wizard
> *Duration*: 5 rounds
> *Range*: Close
> 
> *Source:* Core

An object you touch that weighs no more than 5 pounds becomes fixed in its current location. It can support up to 2250 kg  (5000 pounds) of weight for the duration of the spell.
