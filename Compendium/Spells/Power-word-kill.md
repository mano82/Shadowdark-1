>[!summary]
> *Tier* 5
> Wizard
> *Duration*: Instant
> *Range*: Near
> 
> *Source:* Core

You utter the Word of Doom. One creature you target of LV 9 or less dies if it hears you. 

Treat a failed spellcasting check for this spell as a critical failure, and roll the [[mishap]] with disadvantage.
