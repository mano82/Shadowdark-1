# [[Blind-Deafen|Blind, Deafen]]

>[!summary]
> *Tier* 2
> Priest
> *Duration*: Focus
> *Range*: Near
> 
> *Source:* Core

You utter a divine censure,  blinding or deafening one creature you can see in range.  

The creature has disadvantage  on tasks requiring the lost sense.
