#### *A preserved rat stuffed with an explosive charge and a fuse.*

>[!check] Benefit
>- You can light the bomb's fuse and throw it a near distance. 
>- It explodes in `dice: d4` rounds, dealing `dice: 2d8` damage to everything in near range.
