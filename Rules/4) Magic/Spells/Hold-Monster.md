# [[Hold-Monster|Hold Monster]]

>[!summary]
> *Tier* 5
> Wizard
> *Duration*: Focus
> *Range*: Near
> 
> *Source:* Core

You paralyze one creature you can see within range. If the target is LV 9+, it may make a STR check vs. your last [[spellcasting]] check at the start of its turn to end the spell.
